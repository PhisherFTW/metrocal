Demo 1 Faceit Nuke (team_pertz 11-16 team_metro)

Focus areas:

  1. Use your weapons in positions for them to succeed such as the pistols and smgs at close range and Rifles at longer ranges.
  2. When you suspect the enemies are ecoing or doing a light buy, keep your distance and don’t get close.
  3. Make sure you lock your crosshair onto the enemy before you start spraying because I noticed a few times you would start spraying before your crosshair was on the enemy making it much much more difficult for you to land the kill.
  4. Your pathing and movement could use some work I noticed a few times you would bump into a wall/teammate by accident which messed you up a little bit also sometimes you were too close to your teammates which caused you to die instantly if the enemy had a good spray.
  5. When clearing angles you have the idea down and your crosshair placement is decent but you are rushing things often your crosshair placement would be good but you would move onto the next angle too quickly, which caused the enemy to peek the angle you were holding and kill you just after you moved on, just slow things down a little bit and focus on perfecting your peeks instead of trying to make things quicker, that will come with time.
  6. Getting a little kill-hungry a few times during that game, I completely understand the urges but you have to fight against it, because being kill hungry sure it will sometimes pad your scores but at the end of the day that doesn’t matter its your individual improvement that matters, ontop of just padding your score if you are too kill hungry it can lead to you having worse economy if you die while going for kill and in bad cases lead to your team losing rounds that you would have won if you were not greedy.
  7. Early rotate, this only happened once during this game but it is something to look out for because it is one of the easiest ways to throw a round in csgo is by getting too many people to leave a site wide open and once the Terrorists have the bomb down and are in good afterplant situations its almost impossible to mount a solid retake so in most cases you would want to put up a good fight before the bomb goes down.
  8. Playing the same angle over and over again especially on CT side is a terrible idea as it allows for the enemies to figure out ways to counter you this includes prefiring and knowing your exact position, I would never recommend sitting in a position that the enemies know you will be in as this can lead to you dying very quickly from an awp peek or a prefire as the terrorists have better weaponry and this completely throws away your CT advantage which is the choice to choose a different position every single round without the enemies knowing.
